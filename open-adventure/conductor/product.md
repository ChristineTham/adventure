# Product Guide: Open Adventure

## Project Goal
A modern forward-port of the classic Crowther/Woods Colossal Cave Adventure 2.5 game.

## Target Audience
The primary audience consists of **Retro Gamers** who are seeking the classic text adventure experience on modern systems.

## Future Focus
The project will focus on the following key areas moving forward:
- **Maintenance:** Fixing bugs and maintaining CI/CD stability to ensure the game remains playable.
- **Portability:** Continuing to refactor the codebase to make it easier to translate to other languages in the future.
- **QoL Features:** Introducing quality-of-life enhancements, provided they can be toggled via the oldstyle flag.

## Community Contributions
We maintain a stance of **Strict Originality**. We will only accept community contributions and bug fixes that preserve 100% of the original game's behavior. Any deviations must be strictly guarded.
